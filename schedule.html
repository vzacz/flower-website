How It Works

From Order to Your Door

Our simple weekly cycle keeps your flowers fresh and your schedule predictable.

Place Your Order

Browse our shop and submit your wholesale order anytime during the week. Orders are collected on a rolling basis.

Thursday — We Order the Flowers

Every Thursday, we compile all orders and source the freshest blooms from our growers. This is our weekly ordering day.

Flowers Arrive & Get Prepped

Fresh-cut blooms arrive, are hydrated, quality-checked, and packed with care — ready for delivery the following week.

Monday–Thursday — Delivery

Your flowers are delivered Monday through Thursday across the Bay Area. We'll coordinate timing that works for your business.

Good to Know

Delivery Details

Order Cutoff

Place your order by Wednesday evening to be included in that week's Thursday flower order. Orders after that go to the following week.

Bay Area Delivery

We deliver across the Bay Area — San Francisco, Oakland, San Jose, and surrounding cities. Contact us to confirm your area.

Freshness Promise

Flowers are ordered fresh every Thursday and delivered within days. No sitting in warehouses — straight from the farm to you.
