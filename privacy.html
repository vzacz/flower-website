Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 26, 2026

Green Life Flowers ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers and website visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or place an order.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order or interact with our website, we may collect:

  • Contact information: Name, email address, phone number
  • Business information: Company or business name
  • Delivery information: Street address, city, state, ZIP code
  • Order details: Products selected, quantities, delivery dates, special instructions
  • Communication data: Messages sent through our contact form
  • Email subscription: Email address if you sign up for our mailing list

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Process and fulfill your wholesale flower orders
  • Communicate with you about your order status and delivery
  • Send order confirmation emails
  • Respond to your inquiries and contact form messages
  • Send promotional emails and discount codes (only if you opt in)
  • Improve our website and services

3. Data Storage

Your order and contact information is stored securely. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. We do not collect or store payment card information — all payments are arranged separately upon order confirmation.

4. Cookies & Local Storage

Our website uses browser local storage (similar to cookies) to:

  • Shopping cart: Remember items in your cart between page visits
  • Preferences: Store your cookie consent preference
  • Discount codes: Remember email signup discount codes

We do not use third-party tracking cookies. No advertising networks or analytics services track your browsing activity on our site.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • EmailJS: To send order confirmation emails. Your email address and order details are transmitted to EmailJS solely for the purpose of delivering your confirmation email.
  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google may collect your IP address when fonts are loaded.
  • Supabase: For database services. Your data is stored securely on Supabase's infrastructure.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of promotional emails at any time
  • Decline non-essential cookies/local storage

7. Data Retention

We retain your order information for as long as necessary to fulfill your orders and provide customer support. Contact form messages and email signups are retained until you request deletion.

8. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us
