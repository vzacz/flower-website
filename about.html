12 Hours of Sunlight Year-Round
Ecuador's equatorial location provides consistent, powerful sunlight every day, helping flowers develop larger blooms and richer colors naturally.
⛰️
High Altitude Mountain Farms
Flower farms sit over 9,000 feet in the Andes Mountains. The cool air and intense sunlight produce thicker stems and longer-lasting petals.

World Famous Premium Roses
Ecuadorian roses are prized by top florists worldwide for their large flower heads, vibrant colors, and exceptional freshness and longevity.
